Mavera ("we", "our", or "us") is a word puzzle game designed for children and families. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

üîí Our Commitment: Mavera is designed with children's privacy in mind. We minimize data collection and provide easy data deletion options.

üìä Information We Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

Game data stored in the cloud (Unity Gaming Services):

  • Game progress and high scores
  • Leaderboard rankings (anonymous player ID)
  • Avatar preferences

This data is associated with an anonymous player ID and does not contain personally identifiable information.

Game data stored locally on your device:

  • Language preferences
  • Sound settings
  • Cached game progress

üèÜ Leaderboard Details

Anonymous Player ID: When you first play Mavera, Unity Authentication automatically creates a random, anonymous Player ID (e.g., "abc123xyz"). This ID is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information. It is used solely to track your game progress across sessions.

Username/Nickname: You can optionally choose a display name (nickname) that appears on the leaderboard. This name is chosen by you and should not contain personal information. If you don't choose a name, a randomly generated name will be displayed.

Avatar Selection: You can select an avatar icon from predefined options. These avatars are provided by the app and do not involve uploading personal photos.

Leaderboard Privacy: Your score is automatically submitted to the global leaderboard when you complete puzzles. Currently, there is no option to hide from the leaderboard while keeping your progress synced. If you wish to be removed from the leaderboard, use the "Delete My Data" feature, which will reset your score to zero and mark your entry as deleted.

üìù App Permissions

To function properly, Mavera requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Used solely to display ads via Google AdMob.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check if the device is connected to the internet.

These permissions are used exclusively for advertising purposes and not for collecting personal data.

üì∫ Advertising

Mavera displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Our advertising is configured for child-directed content in compliance with:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • Google's Families Policy
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for users under age of consent

üõ°Ô∏è Child Safety Ad Configuration

Our app implements the following technical safeguards in the ad SDK:

  • TagForChildDirectedTreatment: Enabled - ensures COPPA-compliant ads
  • MaxAdContentRating: Set to "G" (General Audiences) - only family-friendly ads are shown
  • TagForUnderAgeOfConsent: Enabled - provides additional protection for users under GDPR age of consent

üìã Types of Advertisements

Mavera uses the following ad formats:

  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown after completing every 5 categories. These ads appear only after the game displays a clear warning message ("Ad loading...") for 3 seconds, ensuring the user expects the ad.
  • Rewarded Ads: Optional ads that users can choose to watch in exchange for hints during gameplay. These are never shown automatically.
üîî Ad Warning System: Before any interstitial ad is displayed, users see a clear message ("Congratulations! Ad loading...") for 3 seconds. This ensures children are not surprised by ads and prevents accidental clicks, in compliance with Google Play's Families Policy.
Important: AdMob may collect certain device information (such as advertising ID) to serve appropriate ads. This data is handled by Google according to their Privacy Policy. We have enabled "child-directed treatment" in AdMob to ensure appropriate ad content.

üóëÔ∏è Data Deletion

You have multiple options to delete your data:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  • Open the app ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí "Delete My Data" button

Option 2: Email Request

  • If you cannot access the app (e.g., lost device), you can request data deletion by emailing us at mavera.support@proton.me with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  • Please include your Player ID (found at the bottom of the Settings menu in the app) so we can locate your anonymous data. Without this ID, we cannot identify your data to delete it.
  • We will process your request within 30 days.

Data deletion will permanently remove:

  • All cloud-stored game progress and scores
  • Avatar and settings preferences
  • All locally cached data
  • Your Unity Authentication account
Leaderboard Entries: When you delete your account, your leaderboard score is reset to zero and marked as deleted. The entry may remain visible in the leaderboard but is no longer linked to any identifiable user account. This ensures complete anonymization of your previous rankings.

After deletion, you will be signed out and can start fresh if desired.
